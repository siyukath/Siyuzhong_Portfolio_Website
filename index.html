ABOUT

Siyu Zhong is a Chinese artist, illustrator, UI/UX designer and digital designer. She is currently studying in University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Stamps School of Art and Design.

Siyu Zhong is an artist concentrated in illustraiton and digital design. Most of her work are inspired by movies and books.

Defamiliarization, an artistic technique that presents common things in unfamiliar or strange ways and evokes a new perspective, inspired her to apply abstraction and magical-realism to the natural world and distort it to create whimsical scenes.

Siyu is recently working on more digital artworks and designs, including a new project in website design.
